Unholy Death Knight Overview
Unholy Death Knight is a 2-hand, plate wearing DPS specialization that focuses on diseases, pets, and shadow damage. We summon armies of undead minions, infect our enemies with multiple different plagues, and blow up packs of enemies with shadow damage. We are generally a very bursty spec in both single-target and AoE. New Unholy Death Knights start at level 8 instead of level 1 since we are a hero class. All races can play Unholy Death Knights, except Dracthyr and Earthen. Unholy Death Knight is a spec for players who like big burst cooldowns and rewarding AoE gameplay.
