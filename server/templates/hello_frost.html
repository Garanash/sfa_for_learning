Frost Death Knight Overview
Frost Death Knight is a Two-Hand and Dual-Wield,
plate DPS specialization focusing on physical and magical damage.
We can guarantee critical hits on our primary damage-dealing ability Obliterate with Killing Machine, breathe ice on our enemies with Breath of Sindragosa and Frostwyrm's Fury, and engulf our enemies in blizzards with Remorseless Winter. We focus heavily on cleave damage that does not cost single-target damage, excelling in 3-target cleave.
New Frost Death Knights start at level 8 instead of level 1 since we are a hero class. All races can play Frost Death Knights except the Dracthyr. Players who enjoy resource management, reacting to procs, and setting up cooldown windows will enjoy Frost.
